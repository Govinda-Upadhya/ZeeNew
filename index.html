Zeebar Resort

A harmonious blend of modern luxury and Bhutanese tradition, perched atop a hill with sweeping views of the serene river below. Our enchanting retreat invites you to immerse yourself in the perfect fusion of breathtaking landscapes and unparalleled hospitality. Nestled amidst lush greenery, Zeebar Resort offers a sanctuary where the beauty of nature intertwines seamlessly with the elegance of modern amenities. Wake up to panoramic views from accommodations that effortlessly combine comfort with awe-inspiring vistas, providing a visual feast for the senses. As you explore the resort, you'll discover a celebration of Bhutanese tradition woven into the fabric of our design and hospitality. From architectural elements inspired by local heritage to culinary experiences that showcase the richness of Bhutanese cuisine, every detail reflects a commitment to preserving tradition while embracing the comforts of the modern world. Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, a family retreat, or a venue for special events, Zeebar Resort caters to your desires.
